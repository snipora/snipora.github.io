> [!IMPORTANT] Security notice
> Snipora is an open-source project. Official binaries are not code-signed at this stage.
> - ::social/windows:: Windows: SmartScreen may warn before running the installer
> - ::social/apple:: macOS: Gatekeeper may blobk the app initially
> 
> This is normal behavior for unsigned binaries.
